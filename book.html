1. Historical Timeline

The Genesis (1920)

Founded on **August 1, 1920**, following a protest against the exclusion of Sailesh Bose from Jorabagan Club. Suresh Chandra Chaudhuri, Raja Manmatha Nath Chaudhuri, Ramesh Chandra Sen, and Aurobinda Ghosh established the club to represent the people of East Bengal.

The club won its first **Calcutta Football League (CFL)** title in **1942** and its first **IFA Shield** in **1943**. A historic triple IFA Shield win occurred from **1949‚Äì1951**. In 1953, the club toured Romania, becoming the first Indian club to tour the Soviet Union/Eastern Europe.
